My Top Three Skills

During the Fall 2022 semester, I took a Career Development class at NKU. In this class, we did various assessments about our personalities, skills, values, etc. Through the skills assessment we took, I was able to identify some of my top skills.

Observing

Since I was young, I have always been an observer. Over the years, I have improved my observation skills through pursuing my academic career and aging. I am an introverted person, so I prefer to observe rather than act/talk.

Organizing

Another skill I have had since I was young is organizing. I have always kept all of my spaces and school work organized. Naturally, this skill has improved as I've gotten older due to practice within my academics and personal life.

Critical Thinking

Critical thinking is a skill I have developed while I have been in school. A variety of subjects and assignments require you to think critically in order to complete them. Over the years, as situations become more difficult, you need to think more critically.
